2. My Projects

Colombia Adentro

This was my middle-carreer project. Alongside 3 other students, we developed an app for getting to know the country within. As we know, Colombia is full of hidden nature and hidden stories, kept from us due to the country's cultural, political, social and geografical complexities. We wanted to expose certain places and people that had lived through the armed conflict and were now developing projects based on tourism throughout the country. All of us were, and some still are, skeptical about these comunities. However, we wanted to transform this feeling and explore everything Colombia has to offer.

Learn

This is a personal project that's currently being developed. My aim is to reform learning. There is so much we are doing wrong right now education-wise, not even the people who have access to this privilege are learning properly. Do you remember what you learned last week? What you learned in college? How about what you learned in high school. I'm certain you remember less than 10% in terms of lecture content. So pretend you invested 10 years of learning, that means you have already "lost" 9 years! Of course, content is not the only thing we learn through education but, wouldn't it be nice to remember at least 70% of what you learn years from now? I'm mixing aspects of neuroscience, machine learning, and psychology to construct something so that I can spend less time learning each activity and have great recall later on.

Zero Waste

This was my first project in university. I gathered with some friends and developed an app so that we could stop throwing away so much food. It was not easy, though, we spent long nights trying to do stuff that takes a matter of minutes right now. But I guess that's part of learning, isn't it? We had this idea because so much resources were being wasted. Just imagine the amount of food-related waste produced at each home each week, now multiply it by the population of Bogota. It's not sustainable!
